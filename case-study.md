# AI Job Intelligence System

## Executive Summary

Designed an AI-powered job intelligence workflow that automates job discovery, qualification, prioritization, and application support generation.

The system aggregates opportunities from multiple sources, standardizes job data, evaluates role fit using AI-driven scoring logic, prioritizes the strongest opportunities, and generates structured application support materials.

Built using n8n, OpenAI, API integrations, and workflow automation principles.

---

## Business Problem

Job seekers spend significant time manually searching multiple job boards, comparing opportunities, tracking applications, and preparing supporting materials.

This process is repetitive, difficult to scale, and often results in missed opportunities.

---

## Solution

Designed a workflow that automates:

- Job aggregation
- Data normalization
- Duplicate removal
- AI relevance scoring
- Opportunity prioritization
- Application support generation
- Automated reporting

---
